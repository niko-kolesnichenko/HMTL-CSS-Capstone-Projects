Book Bound is a small family business that produces ebooks that are lovingly formatted, typesetted, proofread and corrected. We take advantage of state-of-the-art e-reader and browser technologies and adhere to a high quality standards to help you build and publish your best works.

Why BookBound can be the best choice for you?

Professional-quality typography

We apply modern approaches to ebook styling to ensure professional and consistent typography throughout the whole text. Our level of detail includes even such "small" things like using “curly” quotes, em- and en-dashes, indentations, working (!) footnotes, and the like.

You can be sure that your ebook would look astonishing.

Proofreading and corrections

Our services include careful and complete readthrough of each of your ebooks before their release. We can either point out or fix issues like inconsistent spelling, missing accent marks, or missing punctuation.

State-of-the-art formats and features

Let's leverage ebook technologies together!

Hyphenation support, popup footnotes, high-resolution and scalable vector graphics, cross-device compatible tables of contents, and more - all those features will make your ebook into the unforgettable experience.

Quality covers

Do you want to grab a reader's attention right in their e-reader's "library" section?

Our designers can suggest several high-quality art pieces to create attractive, unique and consistent covers for each of your ebooks.

Up and coming: audiobooks!

We plan to add audiobook creation to our services' roster.
